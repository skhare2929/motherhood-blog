## The Overwhelming Joy

As new parents, there is always a lingering question- are we doing it right? Everything is new to us just as its new to the baby. We both are learning, nobody is prepared for going without any sleep for a whole night, or don't really understand what sleep deprivation is? until one night you felt it for real. Parenting is not always magical, its practical, its chaos, its struggle, its emotional roller coaster, its difficult. But it sure comes with its own joy. When you sit in the room, baby sleeping in the crib and there is silence-  just looking at your creation and thinking about that long journey of nine months, that's when you actually feel- the overwhelming joy.
